What is BMI ?

BMI or Body Mass Index is a measurement that is a ratio of your weight and height. It is a good way to gauge whether your weight is in healty proportion to your height.


Examples of health risks if out of the healthy range

Being overweight can lead to a range of chronic conditions including:

- Type 2 Diabetes
- High blood pressure
- Osteoarthritis
- Cardiovascular disease
